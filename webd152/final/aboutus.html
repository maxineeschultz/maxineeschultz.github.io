Our Story

At the Pacific Trails Resort, we believe that there's a better and more sustainable way to vacation in luxury. Founded in 2001, we have been working hard to carefully craft the most perfect experience for our guests including delicious meals made by our award winning chefs and the finest of wines from all over the world. Our location is simply unbeatable in terms of views, offering the very best sights and activities to truly encapsulate the most remarkable parts of the Bay Area in Northern California.

Our Promise to You

Here at the resort, we work hard so you can relax and enjoy your vacation time. We promote relaxation and serenity for all our visitors. We also aim to create a culture of inclusiveness and belonging where all are welcome. We promise to exceed the expectations of all our visitors and work hard to provide excellent customer service. We embrace trends and sustainability practices to provide our guests with the best resort experience while simultaneously taking care of the Earth. We take any and all feedback into consideration to improve our resort every day.

Our Environmental Mission

We aim to give back to the Earth as much as we can. Our Yurts are safe and efficient structures that use minimal materials to construct and are equipped with lights and outlets powered by solar energy. Though we utilize renewable energy, we ensure that your vacation experience is eco-friendly while also being comfortable and luxurious.
